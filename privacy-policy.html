What this policy covers

This policy applies to personal information processed through the app, including account information, quiz progress, category and gameplay data, local offline content, purchase and subscription status, advertising-related data where applicable, and limited technical data needed to operate, improve, and protect the app.

Information we collect and store

We collect only what is needed to provide the features you use.

Account and profile information

If you sign in using Google Play or another supported sign-in method, we may store:

  • Email address
  • Display name or nickname
  • Unique user identifier
  • Profile image or avatar URL if provided by the sign-in service
  • Account preferences and settings

Gameplay and quiz data

We may store data you enter or generate while using the app, such as:

  • Quiz history and completed sessions
  • Selected categories and difficulty preferences
  • Scores, wins, streaks, accuracy, and response stats
  • Team setup data, team names, and local match settings
  • Downloaded offline packs and related sync status
  • Favorites, bookmarks, hidden questions, or custom preferences
  • Feedback, bug reports, and question suggestions you send to us

Purchase and subscription information

If the app offers paid features, upgrades, or other purchases, we may process and store limited purchase-related records such as entitlement status, product identifiers, transaction references, purchase validation results, and timestamps. We do not store your full card number in the app.

Technical and device information

We may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • App version and device model
  • Operating system version
  • Language, region, and time zone
  • Crash diagnostics and performance information
  • Advertising identifiers where supported and permitted
  • Basic network and sync status needed for updates and downloads

Guest mode and Google Play sign-in

Guest mode

If you use Nova Quiz in guest mode, your gameplay data is intended to stay local on your device. This may include local quiz history, team setup information, offline packs, preferences, and gameplay statistics stored in the app's local database.

Guest mode data may be lost if you uninstall the app, clear app storage, reset your device, or otherwise remove local files.

Google Play sign-in

If you sign in with Google Play, some account and app data may be stored in Firebase or related backend services so we can support cloud sync, restore access across supported devices, manage purchases or premium access, validate entitlements, and improve overall account continuity.

Signed-in use may also allow us to associate your gameplay, purchase, and support records with your account.

How we use information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide quiz gameplay, categories, team battle features, and offline packs
  • Save your settings, stats, and progress
  • Sync data across supported sessions and devices when you sign in
  • Deliver cloud-updated question content and category refreshes
  • Process purchases, validate access, and restore premium features
  • Respond to support requests, bug reports, and feedback
  • Monitor app health, prevent abuse, and improve reliability
  • Measure feature usage and improve design, content, and performance
  • Serve ads where the free version of the app includes advertising

Where and how data is processed

Depending on how you use the app, your data may be processed both locally on your device and in cloud services we use to operate the app. Local guest mode data may be stored in on-device databases or files. Signed-in account data may be processed through Firebase, including database, authentication, analytics, and related infrastructure services.

Some service providers may process data on servers located outside your country. By using the app, you understand that your data may be transferred to and processed in other jurisdictions subject to applicable law and contractual safeguards where available.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers that help us run the app and its infrastructure
  • With payment, entitlement, and platform providers to process purchases and validate access
  • With analytics, crash reporting, and advertising partners where applicable
  • When required by law, legal process, or legitimate government request
  • To protect rights, safety, security, users, or the integrity of the app
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, subject to applicable safeguards

Ads and monetization

Nova Quiz may include advertising in some versions of the app, including Google Mobile Ads or similar ad services. Advertising partners may use device identifiers, app interaction signals, or approximate location derived from IP address, subject to platform permissions and applicable law, to help deliver and measure ads.

You may be able to manage ad personalization settings through your device, Google account, or platform privacy settings.

Data retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including to provide the app, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and prevent fraud or abuse.

Local guest data remains on your device until you remove it, clear app storage, uninstall the app, or use any built-in deletion tools. Account-linked cloud data may remain associated with your signed-in account until deleted, anonymized, or no longer needed.

Some records such as transaction logs, fraud-prevention records, or support correspondence may be retained for a reasonable period where necessary for legal, accounting, tax, security, or operational reasons.

Your rights and choices

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account data, subject to lawful exceptions
  • Object to or restrict certain processing in some circumstances
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
  • Manage permissions, sign-in, ad settings, and local storage from your device or app settings

To exercise a privacy request, contact us at support@novastore.co.za.

Permissions

The app may request limited permissions depending on your device and feature use, such as network access, notifications, or storage access needed for downloads and local content. We aim to request only permissions reasonably necessary for core features.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

Nova Quiz is not directed to children under any minimum age that would require verified parental consent under applicable law, unless expressly stated otherwise. If you believe a child has provided personal information in violation of applicable law, please contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party services such as Google Play, Firebase, Google Mobile Ads, analytics providers, crash reporting tools, and other infrastructure partners. These services may process data according to their own privacy notices and terms.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. Your continued use of the app after an update may indicate acceptance of the revised policy to the extent permitted by law.

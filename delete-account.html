Overview

Important Nova Quiz supports both guest mode and signed-in play. The steps and results are different depending on whether your data is stored only on your device or linked to a signed-in account.

If you use Nova Quiz as a guest, most of your data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in with Google Play, some of your account, gameplay, and purchase-related data may be stored in Firebase or related backend systems so we can support cloud sync, account continuity, and payment-related features.

Guest mode data

In guest mode, your gameplay data is generally stored locally on your device. This may include quiz history, selected categories, team setup details, downloaded offline packs, local stats, and app preferences.

To remove guest mode data, you can usually do one of the following:

1

Use any in-app reset, clear data, or delete local progress option if available in Nova Quiz settings.

2

Clear the app's storage from your device settings. The exact steps depend on your device and operating system version.

3

Uninstall the app. This may remove local app data stored on the device, subject to your platform's behavior.

Because guest mode data is local, we may not be able to delete it remotely for you if it was never linked to an account on our systems.

Signed-in account deletion

If you signed in with Google Play, you may request deletion of the account-linked data associated with your Nova Quiz profile.

This process is intended to remove or de-identify data associated with your signed-in account from our active systems, subject to legal, security, accounting, fraud-prevention, and operational retention requirements.

1

Identify the email address or account used to sign in to Nova Quiz.

2

Send a deletion request to support@novastore.co.za using the subject line: Delete Nova Quiz account

3

Include enough information for us to identify your account, such as your sign-in email address, display name, and any relevant support details.

4

We may need to verify that you are the account owner before processing the deletion request.

What gets deleted

Depending on how you used the app, deletion may include:

  • Signed-in account profile records
  • Cloud-synced quiz history and gameplay stats
  • Stored preferences linked to your account
  • Team or gameplay records associated with your signed-in profile
  • Question suggestions or support-linked records that can reasonably be tied to your account

For guest mode, deletion usually means removing local device data, such as:

  • Local quiz progress and history
  • Offline downloaded packs
  • Saved local settings and preferences
  • Locally stored stats and team setup data

What may be retained

Even after a deletion request, we may retain limited information where necessary for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Transaction or purchase validation records
  • Fraud-prevention and security records
  • Legal, tax, accounting, or compliance records
  • Backup data for a limited retention period
  • Aggregated or de-identified analytics that no longer reasonably identify you

Processing and retention timing

We aim to review deletion requests within a reasonable time. Actual completion time may vary depending on verification needs, system limitations, legal obligations, and whether your data is stored locally, in cloud systems, or both.

Some backup copies or security logs may remain for a limited period before being overwritten or deleted in the normal course of operations.

Need help?

If you need help deleting your Nova Quiz account or understanding whether your data is stored locally or in the cloud, contact us at support@novastore.co.za.
